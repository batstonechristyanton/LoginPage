    Description

    Magma II from the typeface designer Sumner Stone is an upgraded version of the Magma typeface family. The vertical stems in Magma are slightly wider at the ends of the strokes than they are in the middle. This swelling compensates for the tendency to have the strokes appear tapered at the ends because of digital imaging technology, and it adds a both an elegance and a robustness to the character of the typeface which is missing in almost all other sans serifs. This allows Magma to be useful at both small and large sizes, where its fine details become more prominentMagma II is available in eight font weights, from Thin to Bold.
